## Tips when adding words

1. **Length** – Prefer 1–3 words. Long phrases (e.g. “The Story of the People of the Cave”) are harder to give clues for and easier to spot as the imposter.
2. **Recognizable** – Words should be familiar to your audience (e.g. “Laylatul Qadr” for Muslims, “Hijrah” for those who know Seerah).
3. **No overlap** – Avoid the same word in multiple categories so the game doesn’t repeat.
4. **Balance** – Mix easy and harder words in a category so different age groups can play.
5. **Custom categories** – Users can add their own in Settings (e.g. “Family names”, “MSA events”, “Local masjid”). The app already supports custom categories.

## Where to edit

- **Default categories:** `data/categories.ts` – add new objects to the `defaultCategories` array (same shape as existing ones: `id`, `name`, `description`, `words`, `locked`, `isCustom`, `difficulty`).
- **Learn more links:** If you use “Learn more” on the reveal screen, add entries in `data/learnMore.ts` for new words that should have a video or link.
