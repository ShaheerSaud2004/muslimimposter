Features

What's in the app

20+ Islamic Categories

Prophets, Seerah, Ramadan, Quran Concepts, Halal Foods, Masjid Life, Islamic History, and many more. All content is respectful and family-friendly.

Multiple Game Modes

Word + Clue Mode: Give clues about your secret word. Quiz Mode: Answer questions about the word. Special Modes: Blind Imposter and Double Agent for extra challenge.

Pass & Play Design

One phone, everyone plays. Pass it around—no separate devices.

Custom Categories

Add your own categories and words—family traditions, inside jokes, whatever you want.

Beautiful Design

Warm Majlis-style theme with Islamic geometric patterns. Simple layout.

All Features Free

No in-app purchases or subscriptions. Everything’s free.

How It Works

Four steps

1

Setup

Choose number of players (3-20), imposters, and categories. Add player names for a personal touch.

2

Reveal

Each player taps their card to see their role and word. Imposters see "IMPOSTER" instead of the secret word.

3

Play

Give clues or answer questions. Debate and discuss. Vote out who you think is the imposter.

4

Results

See who was right. Celebrate wins and laugh about wrong guesses. Play again!
