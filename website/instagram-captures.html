Khafī v1.1.5

New Update

What's new inside

Collections

We added Collections! They are preset groups of categories so you can start a game faster. Pick “Ramadan” or “Most popular” in one tap instead of choosing categories one by one.

Learn & Share

After each round, a “Learn about [word]” section explains the secret word. Tap “Watch video” to dig deeper or share from the header. Especially helpful for Seerah and Islamic-related topics—learn more about them as you play.

v1.1.5

And other boring updates

Play Again now uses a smoother flow: the screen slides to the next round before content updates, so there’s no brief flash of the next game. New Game and Play Again buttons have been moved higher on the results screen for easier access.

This release also includes general polish and small bug fixes. Back button concept credit: @z1a.khan.
